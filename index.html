About KonvictDev

KonvictDev is a dedicated laboratory for building high-performance, privacy-focused web utilities. Our mission is to provide professional-grade tools for developers and job-seekers without the privacy concerns of traditional SaaS platforms.

Our flagship tool, ResuMe, was built to solve the "subscription trap" of resume builders. It uses client-side rendering to ensure that your sensitive professional data never touches a database.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Feb 2026

At KonvictDev, we prioritize your data safety. Our tools operate on a Local-Only processing model.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal identifiers. Your resume content is stored in your browser's localStorage and nowhere else.

2. Google AdSense & Third Parties

We use Google AdSense to monetize our free tools. Google uses cookies (DART cookies) to serve ads based on your activity. You can opt-out at: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Log Files

Like all websites, we use standard log files for site traffic analysis, which includes IP addresses and browser types, but none are linked to personal identity.
